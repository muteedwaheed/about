Our Story

At DhobiLite, we believe that doing laundry shouldn't be a burden. Founded with a vision to transform the traditional laundry experience, we combine cutting-edge technology with time-honored care to deliver exceptional results.

Our Mission: To provide premium laundry and dry cleaning services that save you time while delivering impeccable results. We're committed to making clean, fresh clothes accessible to everyone through innovative pickup and delivery solutions.

From everyday garments to delicate fabrics, we treat every item with the care it deserves. Our eco-friendly processes and attention to detail ensure your clothes come back looking and feeling their best.

Meet Our Founder

Rajesh Kumar

With over 15 years of experience in the textile and cleaning industry, Rajesh founded DhobiLite to bridge the gap between traditional laundry services and modern convenience.

His passion for quality and innovation drives our commitment to excellence. "Every garment tells a story, and we're here to keep that story fresh and vibrant," says Rajesh.

Under his leadership, DhobiLite has grown from a small local service to a trusted name in premium laundry care, serving thousands of satisfied customers across the city.
